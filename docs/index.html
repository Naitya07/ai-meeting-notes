How It Works

Three steps. All on your machine.

Powered by Whisper and Ollama running entirely on your device. No API keys. No subscriptions to third-party AI services.

🎙️
Step 01
Record or Upload
Live-record your meeting or drop in an audio file. WAV, MP3, M4A — all supported.
→
🧠
Step 02
AI Processes On-Device
Whisper transcribes locally. Ollama LLM structures the notes. 100% on your hardware.
→
📄
Step 03
Get Structured Notes
Industry-specific formatted output: SOAP notes, legal briefs, IEPs, financial summaries, and more.
Zero cloud.  Zero uploads.  Zero risk.
Built-In Compliance

Automatic Compliance

Not bolted on. Not an add-on. Compliance is the architecture — because data that never leaves your device cannot be breached, subpoenaed, or sold.

🏥
HIPAA
Protected Health Information stays on your device. No BAA required. Compliant by architecture, not paperwork.
⚖️
Attorney-Client Privilege
Third-party disclosure doctrine cannot attach. Your privileged communications are never transmitted.
🎓
FERPA
Student education records processed and stored locally. Full compliance with Federal education privacy law.
📊
SEC Rule 17a-4
Tamper-evident local recordkeeping for financial advisors and broker-dealers.
🔒
BIPA (Illinois)
No biometric data collection or transmission. Zero exposure to Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act liability.
🇪🇺
GDPR / CCPA
No data processing agreements needed. Data sovereignty is absolute — it lives on your machine.
